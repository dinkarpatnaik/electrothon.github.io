Rules

1 - Problem statement selection

A formed team shall select a problem statement / theme from the given list under PROBLEM STATEMENTS link in the menu bar.

2 - Idea preparation

Followed by the problem/theme selection, the team shall download the powerpoint template from the SUBMISSION TEMPLATE link in the menu bar.
Note: Participants shall strictly adhere to the given template and shall lead to disqualification without any further intimation in case of failure.

3 - Registration & Idea submission

Team leader shall then register by clicking on the REGISTER button at the top-right corner of the portal and upload the submission ppt along with other details about the team members.
On 10 July 2021, the team submissions shall be closed and a primary analysis and elimination shall be carried out. The selected teams shall be intimated on the whatsapp group on or before 15 July 2021.

4 - Idea presentation

The selected teams shall present their idea through Google Meet on 17 July 2021, followed by which assessment shall be carried out by the judging panel. Note: Schedule for presentation by the team members shall be conveyed a day before on the whatsapp group. Note: Schedule for presentation by the team members shall be conveyed a day before on the whatsapp group.

5 - Results / Awards

The winning teams shall be declared once the assessment process is finished.
Further, interested teams can participate in additional benefits such as access to Internships, industry mentors to work on their products, support for innovation Start-ups, and much more.
